Understanding your hormonal health to help you thrive



Women’s hormonal patterns are dynamic and highly individualized, yet current approaches rely on intermittent measurements that fail to reflect how hormone levels change over time.


Women's bodies are dynamic. Our understanding of them should be too.

About EliVive

EliVive is designed to help women better understand how their bodies fluctuate in real time, offering clarity and support for those navigating hormonal imbalances, fertility challenges, and cycle-related changes.

Our Founders

Martina Marrali

Chief Executive Officer

Martina brings over six years of experience in biotechnology across early-stage company building and clinical development. Currently completing her MBA at Babson College (2026), she previously built R&D infrastructure at Engitix Therapeutics and supported cancer vaccine programs at Gritstone Bio. Her focus on EliVive is driven by her own experience with PMDD.

Liza White, PhD

Chief Technology Officer

Dr. Liza White holds a PhD in Biomedical Engineering from the University of Maine, specializing in sensor systems and microfluidic technologies. Her work focuses on robust sensing platforms for longitudinal data. Diagnosed with PCOS, she brings personal insight into the critical need for better daily hormonal tools.
