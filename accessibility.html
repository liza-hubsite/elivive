Accessibility Statement

This statement applies to EliVive website content.

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Our Commitment

EliVive is committed to providing a website that is accessible to people with disabilities. We continuously improve the user experience for everyone and apply relevant accessibility standards in our design and development process.

Conformance Target

Our target is conformance with Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA, which is the technical standard referenced in the U.S. Department of Justice ADA Title II web accessibility rule.

Accessibility Features

  • Semantic page structure with headings and main landmarks.
  • Keyboard-visible focus indicators for interactive controls.
  • Skip link to bypass repeated navigation.
  • Text alternatives for meaningful images and icon links.
  • Accessible form labels and required-field guidance.

Feedback And Assistance

If you encounter an accessibility barrier, please let us know so we can address it promptly.

Contact us through our updates/contact form and include details about the page and the issue you experienced.
